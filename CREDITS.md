# Credits & data sources

- **WordNet 3.1** — Princeton University. Source of words, parts of speech,
  glosses, and synonym sets (synsets). Used under the WordNet license (free to use
  and redistribute with attribution). https://wordnet.princeton.edu/
- **wordfreq** — Robyn Speer et al. Word-frequency data used for difficulty
  tiering. https://github.com/rspeer/wordfreq
- **Definitions & example sentences** — rewritten / generated with Anthropic's
  Claude models, anchored to WordNet ground truth and validated programmatically.
- **Country / capital / flag data** — a small hand-curated set embedded in the app;
  flags render via Unicode regional-indicator emoji.
